About Me

Learn more about Alvar Svardby

Hello! My name is Alvar Svardby

I come from the beautiful town of Örnsköldsvik, Sweden. I am currently a student at Höga Kusten Theoretical High School (HKT), where I am in my second year. My academic journey at HKT has been both challenging and rewarding, helping me build a strong foundation in various subjects.

I am a quick learner with a natural talent for shaping and executing plans effectively. These skills have enabled me to tackle various projects with confidence and precision. One of my proudest achievements so far is designing and building a DC motor—a project that tested my knowledge and problem-solving abilities. In addition to this, I have also built several websites using HTML and CSS, which has further sparked my interest in technology and design.

Outside of my academic studies, I have a wide range of interests. I am passionate about strength training and appreciate the discipline it brings to my life. My interest in building extends beyond physical fitness—I also enjoy building computers, where I can combine my technical knowledge with hands-on work.

In my free time, you can often find me practicing karate or hiking in the mountains, enjoying the beauty of nature. One of my favorite places to ski is Åre, where I find both challenge and joy in the slopes. I am also interested in stock trading, a hobby that not only fascinates me but also keeps me engaged in the financial world.

Looking ahead, my goal is to one day become the CEO of a company that I have built myself. I am determined to turn my passion for learning, building, and leadership into a successful business where I can create, innovate, and make a meaningful impact.
