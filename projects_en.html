Here I share my experiences and insights as an active investor in the stock market.

I have been trading stocks since 2020, with a focus on the tech and gaming industries. The opportunity to earn money by investing in companies that drive innovation and entertainment captivated me from the start.

My trading strategy is a mix of swing and long-term strategies, allowing me to take advantage of both short-term market movements and the growth potential of promising companies over time.

One of my notable investments was in AMD in early 2024, where I successfully exited the trade after a few months, securing a 20% return. Additionally, I have heavily invested in Take-Two Interactive Software, a company I believe has significant growth potential due to its strong portfolio and upcoming projects.

I stay informed by thoroughly researching companies' performance, future projects, and market trends. My ultimate goal is to consistently beat the market by using a disciplined trading strategy.

However, my journey has not been without challenges. I once used 1:20 leverage on Bitcoin and unfortunately forgot to set a stop-loss, resulting in a total loss of that investment. This experience taught me the importance of risk management and staying vigilant in the fast-paced trading world.
