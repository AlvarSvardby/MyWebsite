My Stocks

Take-Two Interactive Software

I have chosen to invest in Take-Two Interactive Software because they own Rockstar Games, which is currently developing GTA 6. I believe this game has the potential to generate enormous revenue. Additionally, they also own 2K Games, which is producing Civilization VII, a game I also expect to be successful.

The projects I am most excited about are GTA 6 and Civilization VII, both games that I believe will have a significant impact on the company's future.

My plan is to hold my shares in Take-Two Interactive Software until early 2026. It has been important for me to be patient and calm during this investment, as the stock price has fluctuated significantly, with a fluctuation of up to 20 USD over the year.

Avanza Zero

I chose to buy Avanza Zero because it is a fund that tracks the Swedish market and has no fees. This makes it a cost-effective and accessible investment to gain exposure to the Swedish stock market.

Avanza Zero is a safe fund with no fees. It is a good foundational investment for diversifying the portfolio without having to worry about high costs.

I don't have a specific plan for how long to hold the fund, but I plan to keep it for at least a few years. This gives me time to see returns and allow the fund's value to grow over time.

My strategy is to "hodl" (hold) my investments. This means that I do not plan to make any quick changes but instead focus on long-term growth.
